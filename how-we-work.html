Our Process

A clear path from vision to completion.

Every project begins with a conversation. We listen, we plan, and we build — with you involved at every step.

1

Share Your Vision

Tell us what you're imagining. A bigger kitchen? A second story? A brand-new home? We'll help you shape the idea — even if you're not sure exactly what it looks like yet. Everything starts here.

2

Define the Plan

Once we understand your vision, we give it definition. Scope of work, budget, schedule, timeline — this is where the details come together. We don't tell you what you want to hear. We tell you what you need to know.

3

Build Together

We have a shared vision and a solid plan. Now we execute. This isn't just a final step — it's the beginning of a relationship. Our goal is to inspire confidence at every stage.

What to Expect

Your project, step by step.

Here's what the journey looks like from first call to final walkthrough. No surprises — just clear communication at every stage.

Week 1
Initial Conversation
We start with a phone call or in-person meeting. Tell us about your project, your goals, and your budget range. This is a no-pressure conversation — we're here to listen.
Week 1–2
Site Visit & Assessment
We visit your property to understand the space, identify challenges, and start thinking through solutions. For new construction, we review the lot and any existing plans.
Week 2–3
Scope & Budget Alignment
We put together a detailed scope of work and budget. This is where we make sure your vision and your budget are aligned. Honest numbers, no hidden costs.
Week 3–4
Project Kickoff
Contract signed, materials ordered, schedule locked. You'll get access to the 52 Build app so you can track every milestone in real time.
Ongoing
Weekly Updates & Communication
You'll never have to wonder what's happening. Weekly progress updates, photo documentation, and direct access to your project manager through the app.
Completion
Final Walkthrough & Handoff
We walk through every detail together. Punch list items are addressed immediately. You get a complete project file with all documentation, warranties, and photos.
