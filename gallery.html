Lewisburg Barndominium

Lewisburg, TN Custom Home Liberty Valley Rd

A custom barndominium build on Liberty Valley Road in Lewisburg, Tennessee. This project combined the open, flexible layout of barn-style construction with high-end residential finishes — vaulted wood ceilings, polished concrete floors, a chef's kitchen, and thoughtful details throughout.

  • ✓ Custom new construction
  • ✓ Metal roof & board-and-batten exterior

  • ✓ Open concept living & kitchen
  • ✓ Polished concrete floors
